Ping Pong

Rules

Ping Pong games will print out your number, with expception:

  • Number divisible by 3, will become "Ping"
  • Number divisible by 5, will become "Pong"
  • Number divisible by 15, will become "Ping-Pong"

A number of consists the value of:

  • of Ping.
  • of Pong.
  • of Ping-Pong.
